About Me

As a B.Tech student in Electrical Engineering at IIT Hyderabad, I’m in the home stretch of my academic journey. But let me tell you, the ride has been nothing short of exciting. My passion for solving research problems has fueled my journey thus far, and I have relished every challenge that has come my way. My background in electrical engineering has developed a robust foundation in mathematics and the capability to seamlessly integrate ideas from different disciplines.


My research journey has been a diverse one, covering areas such as speech signal processing, image processing, and the application of stateless blockchain technology. Currently, I am working under the guidance of the legendary Prof. Mathukumali Vidyasagar, FRS, exploring the fascinating intersection of stochastic approximation, reinforcement learning, and convex optimization.


As a member of The Global Partnership on Artificial Intelligence (GPAI), I am working towards building an AI community that fosters collaboration and innovation. My diverse background, which encompasses experience in startups (WiSig Networks), government organizations (DRDO), and leading corporations like NVIDIA ,has equipped me with a well-rounded perspective that I bring to the table.

In my quest to make the world a better place, I am an avid supporter of free software and make sure that my contributions to the scientific field are always open-sourced for the world to see. If you’re interested, you can follow my work on Google Scholar and GitHub.

When I’m not working, I love to play cricket, immerse myself in music, and binge-watch TV shows. I’m also an extensive traveler, always eager to explore new places and cultures

I am looking for Ph.D. opportunities under leading researchers in Optimization or Signal Processing. If my profile piques your interest, I would be more than happy to connect and explore opportunities.
